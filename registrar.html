  • Prof. S. K. Sharma has a vast experience of Academia, Research and Development, Human Resource Development, Industries and Administration spread over about three decades. He earned his Ph. D. degree from Kurukshetra University under the noble guidance and joint supervision of Prof. A. P. Sharma (Emeritus Scientist of CSIR, New Delhi) and Prof Shyam Kumar (Dean of Science and Engineering, K.U., Kurukshetra). After starting his career from Kurukshetra University, Dr. Sharma has worked in some of the premier institutions of learning in the region like A.M.U., Aligarh, G.N.D. University, Amritsar, N.I.T, Kurukshetra M. M. U., Mullana and many Engineering Institutions. Dr Sharma has held important academic and Research positions like scientist, Principal and Director etc. successfully. He has been a founder member of many academic/technical institutions in the region. He is a Member of several academic bodies like member of Executive Council of Nuclear Track Society of India, Life member Society for Scientific Values (SSV), Life Member Indian Association for Physics Teachers (IAPT) Secretary, Vigyan Bharti, Haryana etc.
